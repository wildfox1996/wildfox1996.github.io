About us

We share information about Siberian Husky. West Siberian Husky - one of the most popular and common breeds of hunting dogs in Russian country, one of the few native (indigenous), the Russian breeds, them pride. Its popularity is due not only functional and natural beauty, but also excellent working qualities, versatility and remarkable endurance and ability to adapt to a variety of natural conditions and conditions of detention. Widespread belief that these northern dogs bad feeling in an urban environment or in the heat, is not true. Correctly, in our opinion, to consider this breed adapted to extreme continental climate. Thick and dense undercoat "wolf" type Husky allows easy transfer of a variety of climatic and weather conditions. Characteristically, husky dogs of this type with a thick and dense undercoat are also found in tropical regions of Central and Middle Asia (Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia) and even near the equator in Africa.
